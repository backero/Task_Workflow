#RuleWhyTarget
1PriceMeesho buyers are price-first. Rs 99-179 is magic band.Rs 99-179
2CTRTells if people like your thumbnail. #1 ranking factor.≥1.2%
3CVRTells if clickers actually buy. Low = price/image problem.≥2%
4Monthly Clicks50+ clicks/mo = Meesho shows your product.≥50 clicks
5Return RateAbove 20% kills your ranking. Meesho punishes.≤18%
