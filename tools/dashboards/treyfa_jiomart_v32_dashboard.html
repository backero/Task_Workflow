🤖 Part II: How JioMart Works (The 4-Factor Algorithm) ▼

From Chapter 6: You are building a relationship with a computer program. It cares about 4 things only.

Item Completeness — 40% WEIGHT
Fill EVERY blank field. Title, category, description, ingredients, usage, hair type, scalp type, storage, origin, shelf life, gender, age, fragrance, color, pack size, safety. 90% completion = 73% higher ranking.
Performance Metrics — 30% WEIGHT
CTR (click-through rate): target above 2%. CVR (conversion rate): target above 1.5%. Return rate: below 15%. Order defect rate: below 2%. The algorithm sees ALL of this.
Competitive Pricing — 20% WEIGHT
Keep price within 5% of category average. If average is Rs 140-150, stay in range. 10% higher = buried. 20% lower = flagged as suspicious.
Content Quality — 10% WEIGHT
Images, descriptions, reviews, enhanced content. Important ONLY after the other three are done. A beautiful listing with empty attributes is a shiny car with no engine.

📜 Part X: The Truth Manifesto — Read This Every Morning ▼

From Chapter 20: The 10 Truths of Selling on JioMart.

Truth #1
The algorithm does not care about you. It cares about completeness, performance, price, and content. Feed it those four things and it will feed you customers.
Truth #2
There are no shortcuts. Every seller who tried to hack the algorithm got banned or buried. The only sustainable strategy is: better listing, better price, better product.
Truth #3
Your first 4 weeks were practice. The real game starts now. Everything before this was warm-up. From Week 5 onward, every action either makes you money or costs you money.
Truth #4
Rs 149 is your superpower. Not your branding. Not your packaging. Not your story. Your price. At Rs 149, you outcompete 70% of hair oil sellers on JioMart.
Truth #5
Returns are your biggest enemy. Not competitors. Not low sales. RETURNS. Every return costs you money AND hurts your algorithm score. Get it below 15% or nothing else works.
Truth #6
Ads are fuel, not fire. Ads do not create demand. They AMPLIFY demand. If your listing is bad, ads just burn money faster. Fix the listing FIRST. Then add fuel.
Truth #7
Consistency beats intensity. Updating 1 SKU per day for 30 days beats updating 30 SKUs in one day. The algorithm rewards consistency.
Truth #8
Data is your only friend. Feelings lie. Opinions lie. Data tells the truth. If your CTR is 0.3%, your listing is bad — even if YOU think it looks great.
Truth #9
80% of results come from 20% of effort. Your two Hero SKUs (Basil Oil + Neem Oil) will generate 80% of your profit. Spend your time accordingly.
Truth #10
This playbook is worthless without execution. You can read this 100 times. But if you do not follow the daily matrix, if you skip Saturday analysis — this is just paper. Execution is everything.
